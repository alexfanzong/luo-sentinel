LUO SENTINEL · RWA ON INJECTIVE · AGENT × HUMAN COMPLIANCE LAYER

Let the Agent get the prep right, and let a human hold the final gate.

LUO Sentinel is a compliance interlayer between an Agent and on-chain execution. The Agent aligns an RWA action to reviewed regulatory evidence and lays out the prep work; a human gives the final confirmation with their wallet; every step traces back to a concrete source.

01 · The problem

Agents are capable — but some calls should stay with a human.

RWA teams have Agents prepare issuance, transfer and strategy. An Agent is great at filling in a path and stitching plausible-looking fragments into a plan — that's exactly its value. But cross-border regulation rarely has one answer; it's more about source boundaries, classification questions, and the points where a human has to make the call.

What's missing is a layer that keeps both the Agent's speed and the human's judgment intact.

Who does what

The Agent gets the prep right; whether to execute is left to a human.

02 · The product

Sentinel is a compliance interlayer between the Agent and on-chain execution.

It doesn't hand out a "you may issue" conclusion. It does something more solid: align the action to reviewed sources, let the reviewer Agents lay out scope, source and risk, then pass it to a human to confirm.

After a human confirms with their wallet, the system mints a receipt and a handoff. That handoff only lets the downstream Agent prepare counsel-facing materials inside the approved boundary.

06 · Receipt protocol

One line goes on-chain: who accepted which boundary.

receiptHash: 0x...
evidenceHash: reviewed OUSG pack
productRefHash: OUSG-like treasury sample
reviewerWallet: 0x...
decision: proceed allowedDownstreamAction: counsel-prep checklist
forbidden: issuance, transfer, order, strategy, asset movement
network: Injective EVM testnet
value: 0 INJ

The receipt is an accountable, traceable record: a human, under a specific evidence scope, confirmed one bounded round of AI collaboration. It doesn't prove compliance, give a legal opinion, or authorize any asset transaction.
