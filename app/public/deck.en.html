LUO SENTINEL · RWA ON INJECTIVE · AGENT COMPLIANCE PROTOCOL

Let the Agent get the prep right, with every step traceable.

LUO Sentinel is a pre-execution compliance control protocol for RWA Agents. Before an Agent moves toward on-chain execution, it aligns the action to reviewed sources, gets human wallet confirmation, and creates a scope-bound protocol packet another Agent can read.

prepareThe Agent turns scattered regulatory sources into a ready-to-run prep checklist.
verifyEvery conclusion lands on a clickable source, and disagreement stays on the record.
confirmA human signs the final confirmation, leaving an accountable, traceable record.

01 · The problem

Agents are capable — but some calls should stay with a human.

RWA teams have Agents prepare issuance, transfer and strategy. An Agent is great at filling in a path and stitching plausible-looking fragments into a plan — that's exactly its value. But cross-border regulation rarely has one answer; it's more about source boundaries, classification questions, and the points where a human has to make the call.

What's missing is a pre-execution compliance control protocol that keeps both the Agent's speed and the human's judgment intact.

Who does what

The Agent gets the prep right; whether to execute is left to a human.

02 · The product

Sentinel puts the Agent's next step inside a pre-execution compliance protocol.

It doesn't hand out a "you may issue" conclusion, and it doesn't create legal advice. It does something more solid: align the action to reviewed sources, let the reviewer Agents lay out scope, source and risk, then pass it to a human to confirm.

After a human confirms with their wallet, the system mints a receipt and a scope-bound protocol packet. That packet only lets the downstream Agent prepare counsel-facing materials inside the approved boundary.

06 · Receipt protocol

One line goes on-chain: who accepted which boundary.

receiptHash: 0x...
evidenceHash: reviewed OUSG pack
productRefHash: OUSG-like treasury sample
reviewerWallet: 0x...
decision: proceed allowedDownstreamAction: counsel-prep checklist
forbidden: issuance, transfer, order, strategy, asset movement
network: Injective EVM testnet
value: 0 INJ

The receipt is an accountable, traceable record: a human, under a specific evidence scope, confirmed one constrained round of Agent collaboration. It doesn't prove compliance, give a legal opinion, or authorize any asset transaction.
